For this game you will need a partner with his or her own tablet. Make sure you and your partner pick the same number joke. Your screen will have matching numbers, colors and animals.  One player should choose the animal in red square. The other player should choose the animal in blue square. One player will have the first number in an equation. The Other player will have the second number in an equation.  Share your number with your partner.  Find the answer and drag it into the purple square. Work together to solve the equation and learn a new joke.
